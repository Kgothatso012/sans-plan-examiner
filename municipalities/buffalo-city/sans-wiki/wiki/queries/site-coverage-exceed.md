# Query: Can I exceed 60% site coverage?

## Answer
Generally no - 60% is the maximum for Residential 1 in Tshwane.

## Exceptions
- Dual occupancy (family cluster): May apply for 65% with council consent
- Affordable housing projects: Case by case with motivation
- Prior existing non-conforming: May retain if not increasing coverage

## Process to Apply for Exemption
1. Submit motivation letter with application
2. Include storm water management plan
3. Show permeable area compensation
4. Council may approve up to 65% for exceptional cases
