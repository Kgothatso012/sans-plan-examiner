# Clause D5: Ramp Gradient

## Requirement
Ramps for accessible use shall have gradients not exceeding 1:12.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Gradient max 1:12 (8.3%) for ramp length <9m
- [ ] Gradient max 1:15 for ramp length 9-15m
- [ ] Handrails both sides for ramps >1m length
- [ ] Landing at top and bottom of ramp

## Common Violations
- Ramp gradient exceeds 1:12
- No handrails on ramp >1m
- Ramp too long without rest landing
- Level change >15mm at bottom of ramp
