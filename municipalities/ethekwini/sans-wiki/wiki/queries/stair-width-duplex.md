# Query: What is the minimum stair width for a duplex?

## Answer
900mm (Clause D1)

## Exceptions
- Single dwelling under 100m²: 850mm accepted
- Any multi-unit (duplex, townhouse): Strict 900mm required
- Common areas in multi-story: 1000mm recommended

## Additional Notes
- Draw actual measurement from section, not assume from plan scale
- Handrail projections subtract from effective width
- Two storey always requires 900mm regardless of floor area
