# Clause B4: Fire Detection

## Requirement
All residential buildings shall be equipped with smoke detection and alarm systems.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Smoke alarms in every bedroom
- [ ] Smoke alarms in hallway serving bedrooms
- [ ] Interlinked alarms recommended
- [ ] Power supply: battery backup or mains powered

## Common Violations
- Missing smoke alarms in bedrooms
- Smoke alarms placed too close to kitchen
- Battery missing or expired
- No interlinking between floors
