# Clause J2: Concrete Strength

## Requirement
Concrete for structural elements shall meet minimum strength requirements.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Foundation concrete: min 25MPa (C25/30)
- [ ] Structural concrete: min 30MPa (C30/37)
- [ ] Slab on ground: min 25MPa
- [ ] Reinforced concrete: min 30MPa

## Common Violations
- No mix design specified on plan
- Concrete strength <25MPa for foundation
- No cover to reinforcement specified
- No specification for curing
