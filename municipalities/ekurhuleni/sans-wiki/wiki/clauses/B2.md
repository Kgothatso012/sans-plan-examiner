# Clause B2: Fire Resistance

## Requirement
Walls separating occupancy areas shall have fire resistance:
- 60 minutes for walls in residential (A)
- 30 minutes for internal walls
- 120 minutes for walls between A and F occupancy

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Fire-rated walls: 60 min (residential), 120 min (A-F boundary)
- [ ] Fire-rated floors: 30 min
- [ ] Fire doors: self-closing, 60 min rating

## Common Violations
- Open plan ground floor with mezzanine (no fire separation)
- Garage beneath bedroom (no fire wall between)
- Timber frame construction in single dwelling (not meeting 60 min)
- Penetration of fire walls for services (ducts, pipes)
