# Clause A1: Site Orientation

## Requirement
All building plans shall indicate north orientation, sun path, and logical site layout.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] North arrow clearly indicated on plan
- [ ] Sun path direction shown (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Primary living areas oriented north for maximum sunlight

## Common Violations
- No north arrow on submitted plans
- North arrow pointing incorrect direction
- Living rooms oriented south without compensation
