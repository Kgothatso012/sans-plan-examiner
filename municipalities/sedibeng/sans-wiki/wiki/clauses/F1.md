# Clause F1: Parking Requirements

## Requirement
Parking requirements based on floor area:
- Residential: 1 bay per 100m² of habitable floor area
- Minimum 1 bay per dwelling
- Visitor parking: 1 per 5 dwellings (for 10+ units)

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Parking bays: 1 per 100m² habitable area
- [ ] Minimum: 1 bay per unit
- [ ] Bay dimensions: 2.5m x 5m minimum

## Common Violations
- Garage counted as living space, not parking
- Basement parking not shown on plan
- Tandem parking not acceptable (requires turning space)
- Visitor parking omitted in townhouse complexes

## Accepted Deviations
| Scenario | Adjustment | Condition |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Transport Oriented | 0.5 per unit | Within 500m of station |
| Heritage area | Reduced | Site constraints documented |
| On-street parking | Accepted | Council agreement |
