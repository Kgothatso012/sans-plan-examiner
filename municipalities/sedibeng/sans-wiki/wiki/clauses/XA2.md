# Clause XA2: Glazing

## Requirement
Glazed elements shall meet thermal performance and safety requirements.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Glazing U-value: max 3.0 W/m²K (single), 1.6 W/m²K (double)
- [ ] SHGC (Solar Heat Gain Coefficient): 0.25-0.5
- [ ] Safety glass in wet areas and doors

## Common Violations
- No glazing specification on plan
- Single glazing in climate zones requiring double
- No safety glass in doors and wet areas
