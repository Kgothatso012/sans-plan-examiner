**Reference:** QS-2026-001

> ⚠️ **MUNICIPALITY NOTE:** This property falls under **Emfuleni Local Municipality (ELM)** — Sedibeng District, NOT the City of Johannesburg. All development controls referenced in this report are based on the Emfuleni Spatial Planning and Land Use Management By-Law and applicable Residential 1 controls. *Verify against official zoning certificate once received.*

---

## 1. REVISION MISMATCH

**Finding:** Drawing set shows Revision 03A (Dec 2019), yet the title block or cover documentation may reference an earlier Revision 01. Inconsistency between revision levels across sheets creates ambiguity about which set represents the current proposed development.

**Requirement:** All 5 sheets must be on the same revision level. Confirm the applicable set for the current application.

**Action Required:** Architect to consolidate all sheets to a single, current revision level and update the title block on all sheets.

---

## 2. MISSING BUILDING LINE DIMENSIONS

**Finding:** No building line dimensions were identified on the site plan or floor plans indicating the setback from the erf boundary to the proposed structure. This is a mandatory requirement for municipal submission.

**Applicable Control (Emfuleni — Residential 1):**
- Street building line: 3.0m minimum
- Side building line: 1.0m minimum (single storey)
- Rear building line: 3.0m minimum

*Verify against erf-specific Annexure L conditions and title deed restrictions once received.*

**Action Required:** Add building line dimension call-outs to site plan showing all setbacks from erf boundaries. Confirm compliance with the above minimums or identify if a relaxation application is required.

---

## 3. MISSING FORMAL COVERAGE & FAR SCHEDULE

**Finding:** No formal coverage or Floor Area Ratio (FAR) calculation schedule was identified on the drawings. The erf size is not confirmed on the drawings, and no site coverage percentage or FAR value is stated.

**Applicable Control (Emfuleni — Residential 1):**
- Coverage: 60% maximum
- FAR: To be confirmed against Emfuleni scheme (estimated ~0.4 based on Gauteng provincial model)
- Height: 2 storeys / 8m maximum

**Action Required:**
1. Confirm erf size from SG diagram or title deed
2. Calculate total building footprint ÷ erf area = coverage %
3. Calculate total floor area ÷ erf area = FAR
4. Present both on a formal "Development Parameters Schedule" on Sheet 1

---

## 4. DOOR AND WINDOW SCHEDULE — COLOR / SPECIFICATION DISCREPANCY

**Finding:** The floor plans specify windows as "black powder coated large section aluminium and clear glass" while the elevation drawings may show a grey powder coat finish. This inconsistency between plan and elevation must be resolved.

**Quoted Note:** *"Black vs grey powder coat inconsistency noted between floor plan and elevation drawings."*

**Action Required:** Architect to standardise the window and door specification across ALL sheets. Confirm final colour/finish on schedule and ensure elevations reflect the same specification.

---

## 5. BOUNDARY WALL HEIGHT — EXCEEDS 2.1m LIMIT

**Finding:** The drawings propose 2.4m high stone screen walls / boundary walls within the property boundaries. The applicable NBR and municipal limit for boundary walls without relaxation is **2.1m measured from Natural Ground Level**.

**Applicable Standard:** SANS 10400 / NBR Part G and Emfuleni scheme building line controls.

**Note from drawings:** "1.2m high brick wall with granite stone top above" and stone cladding finishes observed on boundary walls within plot boundaries.

**Action Required:**
- **Option A (Preferred):** Redesign boundary walls to a maximum of 2.1m above NGL
- **Option B:** Prepare a formal relaxation/motivation application for walls exceeding 2.1m, submitted with the building plan application

**Estimated additional cost (if Option B):** R2,500 – R4,000 (see Quotation Phase 1, Item 1.5)

---

## 6. MISSING FIRE COMPLIANCE ELEMENTS

**Finding:** The drawings as reviewed do not show:
- Smoke detectors
- Fire extinguisher positions and locations
- Emergency lighting specification
- Fire master plan notes

**Applicable Standards:** SANS 10400-N (Fire Protection) and NBR Part N

**Action Required:** Add the following to the drawings:
- Smoke detector positions on all floor plans (interconnected preferred)
- Fire extinguisher locations marked in kitchen, garage, and utility areas
- Fire safety notes on plan (e.g., "Fire extinguisher to be provided in kitchen and garage per SANS 10400-N")
- Fire door specification where required (e.g., bedroom >4.5m from main exit)

---

## 7. MISSING SANS 10400-C DIMENSIONAL COMPLIANCE CHECK

**Finding:** Habitable room sizes, ceiling heights, window dimensions, and ventilation calculations were not verified against SANS 10400 requirements on the drawings received.

**Applicable Requirements:**
| Item | Standard | Requirement |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Habitable room size | SANS 10400-C | Min 6m² floor area, min 2m headroom |
| Ceiling height | SANS 10400-C, Table 2 | Min 2.4m for habitable rooms |
| Window size (habitable) | SANS 10400-N / T | Min 10% of floor area for lighting |
| Ventilation | SANS 10400-T | Min 5% of floor area for ventilation |
| Stair dimensions | SANS 10400-M | Max 200mm rise, min 250mm tread, min 2.1m headroom |

**Action Required:** Architect to complete a dimensional compliance check and confirm all habitable rooms, ceiling heights, windows, and stairs comply. Add a "SANS 10400 Compliance Schedule" to the drawings.

---

## 8. MISSING DISABLED ACCESS COMPLIANCE STATEMENT

**Finding:** No SANS 10400-E (Facilities for Disabled Persons) compliance statement or notation appears on the drawings.

**Note:** For private single-family residential dwellings, full disabled access compliance is generally not mandated. However, a formal deviation or statement noting this must be included in the submission.

**Action Required:** Add a note to the drawings: *"Facilities for disabled persons compliance requirements per SANS 10400-E are not applicable to this single residential dwelling"*, signed by the competent person.

---

## 9. MISSING TITLE DEED / SG DIAGRAM REFERENCES ON DRAWINGS

**Finding:** The drawings received do not contain the erf/stand number, title deed reference, or SG diagram number in the title block or site plan.

**Action Required:** Architect to add the following to the title block on Sheet 1:
- Erf / Stand number
- Title deed reference
- SG diagram reference
- Zoning (confirmed from zoning certificate)
- Zoning certificate date (must be < 60 days old at submission)

---

## 10. ADDITIONAL OBSERVATIONS

### 10.1 — Energy Efficiency (SANS 10400-XA)
No energy compliance calculations or thermal performance specifications were identified on the drawings. An Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) or XA rational assessment may be required by Emfuleni municipality upon submission.

**Action Required:** Confirm with Emfuleni whether XA certificate is required at submission stage. Estimated cost: R5,000 – R8,000 (see Quotation exclusions).

### 10.2 — Swimming Pool
The drawings include a pool and reflecting pond. Pool safety barriers (SABS 1390 / SANS 10400) must be shown and noted.

**Note on drawings:** *"Pond max depth 300mm, to comply with NBR and local by-laws"*

**Action Required:** Add pool safety barrier/ fence specification if pool depth exceeds 300mm, per NBR requirements.

### 10.3 — Rainwater / Stormwater
Rainwater drain collection points are noted on drawings but no formal stormwater disposal plan was identified.

**Action Required:** Confirm connection to municipal storm water system or approved on-site disposal. Add stormwater notes if required by Emfuleni.

### 10.4 — Professional Team Registration
The title block should include the SACAP/ECSA registration numbers of the architect and engineers involved. These must be verified and stamped on the drawings.
