## SCOPE OF SERVICES

We have reviewed the existing drawing set (5 sheets, Rev 03A, dated December 2019) for the proposed Chokoe Mansion and have identified a number of deficiencies that resulted in the application not being approved.

We propose the following professional services to bring this application to approval:

---

### PHASE 1 — APPLICATION REVIEW & DEFICIENCY RESOLUTION

| # | Item | Description | Fee (ZAR) |
|---|------|-------------|-----------|
| 1.1 | **Municipal authority confirmation** | Confirm property falls under Emfuleni Local Municipality (ELM). Obtain zoning certificate and confirm applicable development controls (Sedibeng District). | R 2,500 |
| 1.2 | **Zoning certificate application** | Apply for and obtain official zoning certificate (< 60 days old required for submission). | R 1,500 |
| 1.3 | **Title deed & SG diagram** | Obtain from client or conduct title search. Verify erf boundaries, servitudes, and conditions. | R 2,000 |
| 1.4 | **SANS 10400-A Form 1 preparation** | Complete the official application form with all required fields, owner details, architect and engineer particulars. | R 1,500 |
| 1.5 | **Boundary wall relaxation assessment** | Assess the 2.4m stone screen walls against the applicable 2.1m boundary wall limit. Prepare motivation/relaxation application if required. | R 2,500 |
| 1.6 | **Client consultations (×4)** | Meetings/calls with client to gather documentation, clarify requirements, and present findings. | R 3,000 |

**Phase 1 Subtotal:** | R 13,000 |
