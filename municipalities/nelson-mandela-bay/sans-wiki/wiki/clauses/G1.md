# Clause G1: Window Area (Natural Light)

## Requirement
Every habitable room shall have window area not less than 10% of floor area.
Light shall be obtainable from the open sky.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Window area: min 10% of floor area
- [ ] Window must face open sky (not courtyard walls)
- [ ] Glass area, not frame area

## Common Violations
- Dark kitchen extension with small windows
- Bedrooms on north side of deep plan (winter light poor)
- High-level windows only (not providing usable light)
- Skylights in roof void - counted but obstructed by insulation

## Accepted Deviations
| Scenario | Adjustment | Condition |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Bay windows | Count actual glass area only | No obscure glass |
| Roof windows | 50% effectiveness | Must be on habitable side |
| Secondary light via internal door | Not accepted | Must be external window |
