# Clause J1: Foundation Requirements

## Requirement
Foundations shall be designed for soil classification and loadbearing capacity.
Minimum foundation depth: 600mm below ground level in expansive soils.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Soil classification report required for sites > 500m²
- [ ] Foundation depth: min 600mm in expansive soils
- [ ] Foundation type appropriate to soil bearing capacity

## Common Violations
- No soil report when required (becomes compliance issue later)
- Strip footings on rock (overdesigned) or on clay (underdesigned)
- Depth less than 600mm in Highveld black clay areas
- Building on made ground without proper foundation design

## Tshwane Specific
- Highveld areas: black clay (expansive) - min 800mm strip
- Rocky areas: can reduce to 300mm
- Fill areas: need geotechnical report
