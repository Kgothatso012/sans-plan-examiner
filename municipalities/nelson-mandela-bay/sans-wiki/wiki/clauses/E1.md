# Clause E1: Room Sizes

## Requirement
Minimum floor areas:
- Bedrooms: 6.5m² (single), 10m² (double)
- Living rooms: 12m²
- Kitchen: 4m² (if separate)
- Bathrooms: No minimum in residential, but must be functional

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Bedroom floor area: min 6.5m² single, 10m² double
- [ ] Living room: min 12m²
- [ ] Room width: min 2.1m (to allow furniture movement)
- [ ] Ceiling height: per clause A2

## Common Violations
- "Bedroom" under 6.5m² sold as bedroom
- Living room as passage (can't isolate)
- Kitchen in corner of living room (not separate)
- No borrowed light to internal rooms
