# Clause XA3: Building Orientation

## Requirement
Building orientation shall be designed to maximize solar gain in winter and minimize in summer.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] North-facing windows for passive solar
- [ ] East and west windows minimized or shaded
- [ ] Outdoor living areas on north side preferred

## Common Violations
- All windows on west-facing wall
- No consideration for solar orientation
- Large glass areas on east/west without shading
