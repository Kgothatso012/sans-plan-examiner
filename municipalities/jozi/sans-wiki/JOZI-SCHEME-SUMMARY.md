# CITY OF JOHANNESBURG LAND USE SCHEME — SUMMARY

## Official Documents
- **Scheme:** Johannesburg Town Planning Scheme, 2018
- **By-Law:** City of Johannesburg Metropolitan Municipality Spatial Planning and Land Use Management By-Law, 2016
- **NBR:** National Building Regulations (SABS X2001)
- **SPLUMA:** Spatial Planning and Land Use Management Act, 2013

---

## Key Differences: COJ vs Tshwane

| Item | COJ (Johannesburg) | Tshwane |
|------|--------------------|---------|
| Primary Scheme | Johannesburg TPS 2018 | Tshwane Land Use Scheme 2024 |
| Coverage (Res 1) | 60% default | 60% (Zone 5) |
| Height (Res 1) | 2 storeys or 8m | 10m (Zone 10) |
| FAR | 0.4–0.5 (varies by zone) | 0.3 |
| Building Line (Street) | 3m default | 3m |
| Parking | 1 bay/dwelling (Res 1) | 1 bay/dwelling |
| Height relaxations | Council resolution required | By-law provision |

> ⚠️ **NOTE:** These are indicative values. Always verify against the erf's specific zoning and any Annexure conditions.

## COJ BUILDING PLAN SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS

### Required Documents
1. ✅ SANS 10400-A Form 1 (completed, signed, dated)
2. ✅ Zoning certificate (not older than 60 days)
3. ✅ Surveyor General (SG) diagram
4. ✅ Title deed (to check encumbrances)
5. ✅ Power of Attorney (if applicable)
6. ✅ Architectural drawings (scaled, dated, named)
7. ✅ Site development plan (1:200 or 1:500)
8. ✅ Floor plans (all levels)
9. ✅ Elevations (N, S, E, W)
10. ✅ Sections
11. ✅ Structural engineer's drawings and calculations
12. ✅ Electrical installation certificate
13. ✅ Energy performance certificate (SANS 10400-XA)
14. ✅ Storm water plan
15. ✅ Drainage plan
16. ✅ Fire installation schedule
17. ✅ Glazing schedule (SANS 10400-N)
18. ✅ Correct fees paid (COJ scale of charges)

### COJ-Specific Requirements
- All drawings must be on **A1 size minimum** for submission
- Plans must be submitted **digitally** (PDF) via the COJ e-services portal
- Plans must have architect's and engineer's **SACAP registration numbers** and stamps
- **Fire department approval** required for buildings >3 storeys or >2,000m²
- **Heritage approval** required if property is in a heritage zone or has heritage overlay
- **Environmental authorization** required for certain site disturbances
