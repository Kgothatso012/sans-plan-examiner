## KEY PROCEDURES

1. **Rezoning (Item 44):** Apply to Municipal Planning Tribunal
2. **Consent Use (Item 46):** Apply to City; 30-day comment period
3. **Departure (Item 45):** For building line/height relaxations
4. **Removal of Restrictive Conditions (Item 48):** Apply to High Court or Townships Board
5. **Site Development Plan (Item 123):** Required for GR1, consent uses, and specific zones

## SPECIAL CONTROLS

- **Heritage:** NHBRC + Heritage Western Cape — HWC permit required before/during building plan process
- **Water Restrictions:** Cape Town water restrictions zone
- **Coastal Management:** Coastal management overlay controls apply
- **Wind Mitigation:** GR2–GR6 require wind mitigation measures
- **Noise Mitigation:** CO1/CO2 may require noise mitigation

## KEY DIFFERENCES vs OTHER MUNICIPALITIES

| Item | Cape Town (CoCT) | Tshwane (Pretoria) | COJ (Johannesburg) |
|------|------------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Legal Framework** | Municipal Planning By-Law 2015 + LUPA 2014 | Tshwane Town-planning Scheme, 2001 (Rev 2015) | Johannesburg Town Planning Scheme 2018 |
| **FAR (Residential 1)** | 1.0 (erf ≤650 m²) | 0.3 | 0.4 |
| **Coverage (Residential)** | 60% | 60% | 60% |
| **Height (Residential)** | 8–9 m (2–3 storeys) | 10 m (3 storeys) | 8 m (2 storeys) |
| **Street BL (Residential)** | 1.0–6.0 m (varies by erf size) | 3.0 m | 3.0 m |
| **Heritage Authority** | Heritage Western Cape (HWC) | SAHRA / Provincial Heritage | City of Johannesburg Heritage |
| **Provincial Overlay** | LUPA (Act 3 of 2014) | GDARF (Reg 891) | MPRDA |
