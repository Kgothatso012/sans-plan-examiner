# Clause B3: Means of Egress

## Requirement
Every building shall have sufficient means of egress to allow safe escape in case of fire.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Minimum two exits for buildings >150m²
- [ ] Travel distance to exit: max 45m (open plan), 30m (corridor)
- [ ] Exit width: min 900mm per person (max 50 persons per exit)

## Common Violations
- Single exit for multi-story building
- Dead-end corridors longer than 15m
- Locked fire doors blocking egress
- Exit through kitchen or garage
