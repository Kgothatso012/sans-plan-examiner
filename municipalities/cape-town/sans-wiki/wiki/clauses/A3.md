# Clause A3: Floor Height

## Requirement
Floor of any habitable room shall be not less than 300mm above the outside ground level in flood-prone areas.
Minimum 150mm above adjacent ground in other areas.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Flood-prone areas: min 300mm above outside ground
- [ ] Normal areas: min 150mm above adjacent ground
- [ ] Measure at lowest point of finished floor

## Common Violations
- Lower ground floor in low-lying areas
- Basement habitable rooms with < 300mm rise
- Driveway levels raising ground, floor not raised correspondingly
- Paving against wall without damp course, floor level too low
