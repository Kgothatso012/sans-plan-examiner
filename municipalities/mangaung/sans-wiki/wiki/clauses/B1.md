# Clause B1: Occupancy Classification

## Requirement
Buildings shall be classified according to occupancy:
- A: Residential (dwellings)
- B: Assembly (places of assembly)
- C: Institutional (hospitals, schools)
- D: Office
- E: Mercantile (shops)
- F: Industrial
- H1-H4: Hotels, guesthouses, etc.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Classification stated on plans
- [ ] Correct sub-category (A1, A2, etc.)
- [ ] Multiple occupancy: highest classification governs

## Common Violations
- Home occupation not declared, changes classification
- Garage attached to dwelling - different classification
- Flat above shop - mixed occupancy not handled
- Airbnb/short-term let without reclassification
