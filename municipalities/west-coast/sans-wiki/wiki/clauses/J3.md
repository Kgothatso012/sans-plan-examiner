# Clause J3: Masonry Strength

## Requirement
Masonry units and mortar shall meet specified strength requirements.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Clay bricks: min 7MPa compressive strength
- [ ] Concrete bricks: min 15MPa compressive strength
- [ ] Mortar strength: min 5MPa (1:1:6 mix)
- [ ] Joint thickness: max 10mm

## Common Violations
- No masonry strength specified
- Using ungraded bricks
- Weak mortar mix (sand only)
- No consideration for shrinkage
