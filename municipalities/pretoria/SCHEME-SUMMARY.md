| **Height** | **10m max** | Clause 26, Table D, Zone 10 |
| **FAR** | **0.3 max** | Clause 25, Table C, Zone 21 |
| **Street building line** | **3.0m** (erf < 1,000m²) | Clause 9–11 |
| **Side building line** | **1.5m** (single storey) / **2.0m** (double storey) | Clause 9–11 |

### Annexure L (Overlaid Areas)
Where an erf has Annexure L conditions, those conditions **override** the standard R1 controls. Always check the title deed and zoning certificate for Annexure L clauses.

### Consent Uses (R1)
- Guest house (≥5 rooms = Hotel, requires council approval)
- Place of instruction
- Place of worship
- Institution
- Medical practice
- Restaurant (consent use only, not by right)

## CONSENT / DEPARTURE PROCESS IN TSHWANE

1. **Consent Use Application** (Clause 46): Apply to Municipal Planning Tribunal (MPT)
2. **Departure Application** (Clause 47): For relaxation of building lines, coverage, height, FAR
3. **Rezoning Application** (Clause 44): Change of zoning (e.g., R1 → R2)
4. **Removal of Restrictive Conditions**: Apply to Gauteng Provincial Government or High Court

### MPT Application Requirements
- Completed MPT form
- Motivation letter
- Site development plan (SDP)
-邻居 (Neighbour) notifications
- Proof of payment (application fees)

## SPECIAL CONTROLS — TSHWANE

- **Heritage:** SAHRA / Gauteng Heritage Authority — permit required before demolition/alteration of heritage buildings
- **Pre-1970 Buildings:** May have special heritage status; verify with Tshwane Heritage Section
- **Floodlines:** Structures within 1:100 year floodline require special approval
- **Slopes:** Erven >15% slope require geotechnical report
- **Mining:** Erven on dolomitic land require stability report
