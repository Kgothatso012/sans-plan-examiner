One place to receive & track your applications

Everyone can securely submit and track their building plan applications from the e-Tshwane service.

Choose from our range of services - online submission, real-time tracking, AI analysis, and digital approval letters.

How It Works
  • Submit your building plans online (PDF format)
  • AI analysis against SANS 10400 clauses
  • Receive instant preliminary feedback
  • Municipal examiner reviews and adds comments
  • Get approval, rejection, or revision request
