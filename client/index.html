One place to submit & track

Everyone can securely submit and track their building plan applications from the e-Tshwane service.

Choose from our range of services — online submission, real-time tracking, AI analysis against SANS 10400, and digital approval letters.

No more queues. No more lost documents. No more waiting weeks for feedback.

How It Works

  • Submit your building plans online (PDF format)
  • AI analysis against 30+ SANS 10400 clauses
  • Receive instant preliminary compliance feedback
  • Municipal examiner reviews and adds comments
  • Get approval, rejection, or revision request
