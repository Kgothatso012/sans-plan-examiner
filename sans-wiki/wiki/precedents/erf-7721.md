# Precedent: ERF-7721-2025 - Rejected (Site Coverage)

## Property
- ERF: 7721, Garsfontein
- Address: 142 Marais Avenue, Garsfontein, Pretoria
- Area: 800m²
- Existing: Existing dwelling (to be demolished)

## Application
- New single dwelling (2 storey)
- Floor area: 520m²
- Site coverage: **68%** (exceeds 60% max)
- Parking: Double garage

## Decision
REJECTED

## Reason
Site coverage of 68% exceeds maximum 60% per SANS 10400-H2.

## Violations Found
| Clause | Issue | Required | Actual |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| H2 | Site Coverage | 60% max | 68% |

## Correction Required
Reduce coverage to 60% (max 480m² on 800m² erf):
- Option 1: Reduce second floor by 80m²
- Option 2: Remove covered patio
- Option 3: Reduce garage size

## Analysis Notes
- Applicant argued: "Previous house had 65% coverage"
- Rejected: Previous approval does not set precedent for non-compliance
- Note: Can apply for 65% max with motivation to Council (rarely approved)
