# Clause D4: Landings

## Requirement
Landings shall be provided at top and bottom of each flight and at door openings.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Landing length: min 900mm
- [ ] Landing width: min stair width
- [ ] Level change at door: max 15mm threshold
- [ ] Landing within dwelling for external stairs

## Common Violations
- Landing shorter than stair width
- No landing at external door (fall hazard)
- Landing slope >1:50
- Step down to garage >2 steps without landing
