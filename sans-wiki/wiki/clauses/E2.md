# Clause E2: Bathroom Requirements

## Requirement
Every dwelling shall have at least one bathroom with toilet, washbasin, and bathing facility.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Minimum one bathroom per dwelling
- [ ] Bathroom floor area: min 2.2m²
- [ ] Toilet compartment: min 800mm x 1200mm
- [ ] Ventilation: mechanical or window

## Common Violations
- Bathroom without window or mechanical ventilation
- Toilet opening directly into kitchen
- Insufficient space around toilet (min 200mm each side)
- Shower without proper drainage
