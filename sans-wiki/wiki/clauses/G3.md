# Clause G3: Light Shafts

## Requirement
Rooms without external windows shall have light shafts or mechanical ventilation.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Light shaft min 1.5m² cross-sectional area
- [ ] Light shaft extends to roof or open air
- [ ] Or mechanical ventilation system installed

## Common Violations
- Bathroom without window or light shaft
- Internal room with no ventilation provision
- Light shaft too narrow to be effective
