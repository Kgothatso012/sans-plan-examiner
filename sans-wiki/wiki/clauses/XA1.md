# Clause XA1: Thermal Performance

## Requirement
Building envelope shall meet thermal performance requirements to reduce energy consumption.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Wall insulation: R-value min 1.0 m²K/W
- [ ] Roof insulation: R-value min 2.0 m²K/W
- [ ] Energy performance calculation for >40m²

## Common Violations
- No insulation specification
- Insulation R-value below minimum
- Thermal bridging not addressed
