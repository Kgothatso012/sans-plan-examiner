# Clause H1: Building Height

## Requirement
Building height shall be limited according to zone and occupancy classification.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Residential 1 (dwelling): max 2 storeys
- [ ] Height measured from lowest point of natural ground
- [ ] Architectural features: max 1m above roof line
- [ ] Cellar: counted as storey if floor >1m below ground

## Common Violations
- Three storey residential without approval
- Height exceeding zone regulations
- Mezzanine level counted as third storey
- Water tanks exceeding height limit
