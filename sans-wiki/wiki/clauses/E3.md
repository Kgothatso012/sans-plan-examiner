# Clause E3: Kitchen Requirements

## Requirement
Every dwelling shall have a kitchen area for food preparation.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Kitchen floor area: min 5m²
- [ ] Work surface height: 850-900mm
- [ ] Ventilation: extractor fan required
- [ ] Electrical: dedicated circuit recommended

## Common Violations
- Kitchen without extractor ventilation
- Work surface too low (<850mm)
- No provision for stove/oven space
- Kitchen window too small
