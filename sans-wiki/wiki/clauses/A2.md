# Clause A2: Ceiling Height

## Requirement
Ceiling height for habitable rooms shall be not less than 2400mm.
Ceiling height for non-habitable rooms shall be not less than 2100mm.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Habitable room ceiling: min 2400mm
- [ ] Non-habitable room ceiling: min 2100mm
- [ ] Measurement from finished floor to underside of ceiling

## Common Violations
- Loft conversions with ceiling < 2400mm in bedroom areas
- Flat ceiling in kitchen extension below 2400mm
- Bulkhead above stairwell reducing headroom < 2400mm
