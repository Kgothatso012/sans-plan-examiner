# Clause G2: Ventilation

## Requirement
Every habitable room shall have openable area not less than 5% of floor area.
Where mechanical ventilation provided: equivalent to 5% natural.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Openable window area: min 5% of floor area
- [ ] Windows must be openable to full 5%
- [ ] Cannot rely on trickle vents (insufficient)

## Common Violations
- "Ventilation" shown as fixed louvre (not openable)
- High-level windows only (not usable)
- Window in bathroom opening to enclosed courtyard
- Air conditioning "satisfying" natural ventilation (not accepted)
