# Clause H2: Site Coverage

## Requirement
Site coverage for residential buildings shall not exceed 60% of site area.
Includes all covered areas (buildings, carports, covered walkways).

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Total covered area: max 60% of site
- [ ] Include: main building, outbuildings, carports, covered patios
- [ ] Exclude: uncovered driveways, garden areas, pools

## Common Violations
- Boundary-to-boundary building with minimal garden
- Outbuilding counted incorrectly (sometimes excluded incorrectly)
- Ground floor extending to 60%, upper floor adding more
- Carport not counted - is a violation
