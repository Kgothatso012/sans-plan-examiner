# Clause D3: Handrail Height

## Requirement
Height of handrail shall be not less than 900mm and not more than 1000mm above the pitch line of stairs.
Handrail must be continuous on at least one side.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Handrail height: 900mm - 1000mm
- [ ] Measured from pitch line (angle bisector)
- [ ] Continuous on at least one side

## Common Violations
- Handrail at 850mm (too low)
- Handrail at 1100mm (too high)
- Gaps in handrail (breaks in flight)
- Only on open side, not required closed side
- Handrail not returning to wall at ends

## Accepted Deviations
| Scenario | Adjustment | Condition |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Children's use | 600mm - 700mm | Educational buildings |
| Step < 1m wide | One side only | Single dwelling |
