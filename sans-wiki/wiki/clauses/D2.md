# Clause D2: Stair Rise

## Requirement
Maximum rise of a step shall be 190mm.
Maximum number of risers in a single flight: 16.
Maximum 3 flights without landing.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Individual riser height: max 190mm
- [ ] Consecutive risers: uniform within 5mm tolerance
- [ ] Flight length: max 16 risers between landings

## Common Violations
- Attic conversions with steep stairs (220mm+ risers)
- Mezzanine stairs exceeding 190mm per step
- Inconsistent riser heights in same flight
- More than 16 steps without landing (safety issue)

## Accepted Deviations
| Scenario | Adjustment | Condition |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Existing buildings | Max 200mm | Conversion/refurbishment |
| Alternative access | Max 200mm | Lifter or ramp provided |
| External stairs | Max 210mm | Non-main entrance |
