# Clause F3: Turnaround Space

## Requirement
On-site parking areas shall provide adequate turning space for vehicles.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Turnaround: min 9m diameter or 3-point turn
- [ ] No reversing onto public road preferred
- [ ] Clear access to parking from street
- [ ] Visibility at exit to street

## Common Violations
- No turnaround space provided
- Must reverse onto main road
- Turning radius too tight for standard car
- Gate too narrow for vehicle
