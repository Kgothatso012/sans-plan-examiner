# Clause H3: Boundary Wall Height

## Requirement
Boundary walls shall be limited in height to protect visual amenity and safety.

## Measurable Criteria
- [ ] Front boundary wall: max 1.8m
- [ ] Side and rear boundary wall: max 2.1m
- [ ] Pillars max 2.4m (including decorative elements)
- [ ] Retaining walls: case by case

## Common Violations
- Front wall exceeding 1.8m without special permission
- Double story wall on boundary
- Wall too close to intersection (visibility triangle)
- Wall blocking neighbor's light
