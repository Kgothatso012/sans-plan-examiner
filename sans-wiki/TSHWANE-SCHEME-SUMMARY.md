# TSHWANE LAND USE SCHEME 2024 - Residential 1 Summary

## Official Document
- **Name:** Tshwane Land Use Scheme, 2024
- **Adopted:** 8 May 2024
- **Commenced:** 1 July 2024
- **Clause:** 20 (Conditions applicable to Residential 1 properties)

---

## RESIDENTIAL 1 DEVELOPMENT CONTROLS

When no Annexure L applies (default controls):

| Control | Zone | Value | Clause Reference |
|---------|------|-------|------------------|
| **FAR (Floor Area Ratio)** | Zone 21 | 0.3 | Clause 25, Table C |
| **Height** | Zone 10 | **10 metres max** | Clause 26, Table D |
| **Coverage** | Zone 5 | **60% max** | Clause 27, Table E |

---

## Key Limits for Residential 1

### Coverage (Clause 27, Table E, Zone 5)
| Use | Coverage |
|------|-----------|
| Parking Garages | 80% |
| Showrooms/Warehouses | 60% |
| **Dwelling Houses** | **60%** |
| Guest House/Hotel | 40% |
| Other uses | 50% |

### Height (Clause 26, Table D, Zone 10)
- **Maximum Height: 10 metres**
- This equals approximately **2-3 storeys** depending on floor-to-ceiling heights

### FAR (Clause 25, Table C, Zone 21)
- **FAR: 0.3** (meaning total floor area cannot exceed 30% of erf size)
- Example: 600m² erf × 0.3 = 180m² max floor area

---

## Additional Dwelling House (Granny Flat)

Clause 20(2) allows:
- **1 Additional Dwelling House** (granny flat) with Permission
- Max area: 100m² (excluding outbuildings)
- Min erf size: 1000m²
- Must share one wall with main house
- Must comply with development controls

**Grandfathered rights:** Granny flats approved before 29 April 1992 (under Pretoria Scheme) are deemed adopted if:
- Area ≤ 100m²
- Erf ≥ 1000m²
- Common wall with main house

---

## Building Lines (from Clause 9-11)

| Building Line | Default |
|---------------|---------|
| Street building line | 3m (for erf < 1000m²) |
| Side building line | 1m |
| Rear building line | 3m |

*Note: Check Annexure L for specific property restrictions*

---

## Parking Requirements (Clause 28)

For Residential 1:
- **1 parking bay per dwelling house**
- Must have permanent dust-free surface
- Must have maneuvering space

---

## Key Differences: SANS 10400 vs Tshwane Scheme

| Item | SANS 10400 | Tshwane Scheme |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Site Coverage | 60% default | **60%** (Zone 5) |
| Height | 2 storeys | **10m** (Zone 10) |
| FAR | Not specified | **0.3** |
| Parking | 1 bay/dwelling | **1 bay/dwelling** |
| Boundary wall (front) | 1.8m | Not in scheme |
| Boundary wall (sides) | 2.1m | Not in scheme |

---

## How to Use This

1. **Check zoning certificate** - Get the zone from Electronic Zoning Database
2. **Check for Annexure L** - Specific properties may have different controls
3. **Apply default controls** if no Annexure L:
   - Coverage: 60% max
   - Height: 10m max
   - FAR: 0.3 max
