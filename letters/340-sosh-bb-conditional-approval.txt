DECISION: CONDITIONAL APPROVAL

The proposed building plans are APPROVED subject to the following conditions:

CONDITIONS OF APPROVAL:

1. NORTH ORIENTATION
   Submit a revised plan showing north arrow clearly indicated. SANS 10400-A requires all building plans to indicate north orientation.

2. CEILING HEIGHT
   Provide a section drawing confirming minimum ceiling heights of 2400mm in habitable rooms (bedrooms, living area) and 2100mm in non-habitable areas (bathroom, kitchen).

3. PARKING PROVISION
   Indicate on the site plan the location of at least one (1) parking bay. Single dwellings require minimum one parking space per dwelling.

4. SITE COVERAGE
   Submit erf dimensions to verify site coverage does not exceed 60% of the total area. Include all covered areas (building, carport, outbuilding) in calculation.

5. BOUNDARY WALLS
   Submit detail drawing showing boundary wall heights: maximum 1.8m at front, maximum 2.1m at sides and rear.

6. ENERGY COMPLIANCE
   Submit thermal performance calculation or engineer certificate demonstrating compliance with SANS 10400-XA (Energy Efficiency) requirements, OR include insulation specifications on the plan.
